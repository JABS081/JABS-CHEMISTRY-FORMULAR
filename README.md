## SECTION C: CHEMISTRY FORMULAS (Calculable for JAMB)

| Formula Name | Formula | SI Unit | Constants / Values |
|--------------|---------|---------|--------------------|
| Mole concept | \( n = \frac{m}{M} \) | mol | \( M \) = molar mass (g/mol) |
| Number of particles | \( N = n \times N_A \) | (none) | \( N_A = 6.02 \times 10^{23} \ \text{mol}^{-1} \) |
| Molar volume (gas at STP) | \( V_m = 22.4 \ \text{L/mol} \) | m³/mol | \( 22.4 \ \text{L} = 0.0224 \ \text{m}^3 \) |
| Ideal gas equation | \( PV = nRT \) | Pa·m³ | \( R = 0.0821 \ \text{L·atm/(mol·K)} \) or \( 8.314 \ \text{J/(mol·K)} \) |
| Density of gas | \( \rho = \frac{PM}{RT} \) | kg/m³ | – |
| Graham's law of diffusion | \( \frac{r_1}{r_2} = \sqrt{\frac{M_2}{M_1}} \) | (none) | – |
| Concentration (molarity) | \( M = \frac{n}{V_{\text{(L)}}} \) | mol/L | – |
| Concentration (molality) | \( m = \frac{n}{\text{kg solvent}} \) | mol/kg | – |
| Dilution formula | \( C_1 V_1 = C_2 V_2 \) | mol | – |
| pH | \( \text{pH} = -\log_{10} [H^+] \) | (none) | – |
| pOH | \( \text{pOH} = -\log_{10} [OH^-] \) | (none) | – |
| Ionic product of water | \( K_w = [H^+][OH^-] = 1.0 \times 10^{-14} \) (at 25°C) | mol²/L² | \( K_w = 1.0 \times 10^{-14} \) |
| Equilibrium constant | \( K_c = \frac{[C]^c [D]^d}{[A]^a [B]^b} \) | varies | – |
| Acid dissociation constant | \( K_a = \frac{[H^+][A^-]}{[HA]} \) | mol/L | – |
| Base dissociation constant | \( K_b = \frac{[BH^+][OH^-]}{[B]} \) | mol/L | – |
| Faraday's law (electrolysis) | \( m = \frac{ItM}{nF} \) | kg | \( F = 96500 \ \text{C/mol} \) |
| Cell EMF (Nernst equation) | \( E = E^\circ - \frac{0.0591}{n} \log Q \) (at 25°C) | V | \( \frac{0.0591}{n} \) |
| Gibbs free energy | \( \Delta G = \Delta H - T\Delta S \) | J | – |
| Gibbs free energy & EMF | \( \Delta G = -nFE \) | J | \( F = 96500 \) |
| Rate of reaction | \( \text{Rate} = -\frac{d[A]}{dt} = k[A]^x[B]^y \) | mol/(L·s) | – |
| Arrhenius equation | \( k = A e^{-E_a/(RT)} \) | varies | \( R = 8.314 \) |
| Half-life (first order) | \( t_{1/2} = \frac{0.693}{k} \) | s | – |
| Enthalpy change | \( \Delta H = \sum H_{\text{products}} - \sum H_{\text{reactants}} \) | kJ/mol | – |
| Hess's law | \( \Delta H_{\text{reaction}} = \Delta H_1 + \Delta H_2 + \dots \) | kJ/mol | – |
| Heat of combustion | \( q = mc\Delta T \) (calorimetry) | J | \( c_{\text{water}} = 4.18 \ \text{J/g·K} \) |
| Percentage yield | \( \%\text{yield} = \frac{\text{actual mass}}{\text{theoretical mass}} \times 100 \) | % | – |
| Percentage purity | \( \%\text{purity} = \frac{\text{mass of pure substance}}{\text{mass of impure sample}} \times 100 \) | % | – |
| Empirical formula steps | \( \text{moles} = \frac{\text{mass}}{\text{molar mass}} \) | mol | – |
| Molecular formula | \( \text{MF} = (\text{EF})_n \) with \( n = \frac{\text{molar mass}}{\text{EF mass}} \) | g/mol | – |
